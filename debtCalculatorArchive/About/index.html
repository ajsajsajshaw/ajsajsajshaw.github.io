About the Project:

The Debt Calculator was designed to bring economic transparency to the financial burden of college tuition. Student loans can often sound like an abstract thing and can be brushed past -- but they are real (and large) loans that students around the country take out at young ages.

The website allows you to visualize your own student loans for your own financial understanding, but are also added to an archive of student loans that have been submitted to the website. Very little information is shared about the loan, except for the information that emphasizes the weight of the loan on a student, variables like age and total student debt.

Economic backgrounds can largely affect your experience at these schools; this is true in any environment, but the first step to addressing these problems is through transparency of information. What is a step you can take to push forward the conversation? Sharing your own financial journey.
