'Matty In The Morning' Radio Host Matt Siegel Returns, Day After He 'Snapped' On Air

WBZ News // Boston

PAULA EBBEN:

"Matty is back after appearing to quit 'Matty In The Morning' during his radio show live on air yesterday. Matt Siegel returned this morning with an explanation. WBZ’s Nick Giovanni breaks down what had to say,"

MATT SIEGEL:

"I am the biggest of all time. and they said shut up, Matt! Stop talking! Well, I hope you're happy. Because I just stopped talking. Matty out."

Nick Giovanni:

"Siegel had said on air yesterday that management had spoken to him about comments he made during the show, regarding singer Demi Lovato's announcement that they now identify as non-binary."

MATT SIEGEL:

"Well, I was really having a good time, and then I, you know, I stumbled onto a topic that is a sensitive one, with Demi Lovato, and I was upset with her and her statement about the pronouns. I thought it was ridiculous, and I talked about it, and I told jokes because that’s what we do."

MATT SIEGEL:

"That bothers me that some people thought this is just a gag. This is not a gag."

Nick Giovanni:

"Despite appearing to quit, he told listeners less than 24 hours later that conversations continued behind the scenes that led to his return."

MATT:

"I talked to my wife at length about it, and I talked with the company, and the company told me that… they basically said “Please don’t quit.” And they said I have total support from them and freedom to say what I want."

Nick Giovanni:

"Siegel added if he can’t say what he is thinking within traditional radio boundaries, he doesn’t want to do it anymore. For now, he says he’s back. Reporting in Medford, I’m Nick Giovanni, WBZ News."
